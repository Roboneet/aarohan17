The Aarohan Sample Paper Challenge

Aarohan

A wonderful learning experience

The unique paper crafted by the students under the guidance of an expert panel of professors is designed to rekindle the much needed scientific curiosity. Topics near the boundaries of human scientific achievements would be explained in laymen terms and questions based on the student's existing knowledge follow. Apart from a wonderful learning experience, theres a lot to win!

Certificates to all participants!

Prizes to school toppers!

Top 10 students get an all expense covered trip to APOGEE 2017 along with exciting prizes!

Round 1

The paper will be OMR based.

The duration of the paper will be two hours.

The paper will have one unifying theme based on recent scientific breakthroughs and phenomena, and the questions based on that will be entirely application based and not factual.

The syllabus comprises of the syllabus of the studentsâ€™ respective class. The students will have to get innovative with the basic formulae and improvise.

The marking scheme will be revealed at the time of examination.

Round 2

Two students from each zone would be selected for the final round, that makes a total of 10 students.

An all expense paid trip to BITS Pilani during APOGEE 2017, along with one guardian. Two day all access pass issued to all students.

Each student would be given a particular scientific topic or a research area, one month prior to the second round.

Students are supposed to gather knowledge, relevant data, notes, breakthroughs and current happenings related to that topic. Help from teachers is encouraged.

Each student would have to present their topic which would be followed by constructive group discussion in the presence of a panel.
